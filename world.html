🎣 FISHING — ON THE PLATFORM

Walk to the ⚓ fishing platform (on the lake) → press E → your unit jumps onto it. Use 🚪 EXIT PLATFORM (top) to leave.
1. AIM & CAST — press E, aim the target with the ARROW KEYS anywhere on the water, press E to cast.
2. STRIKE — when it flashes STRIKE!, press E fast.
3. REEL — use ↑ / ↓ arrows to move the green band & keep the 🐟 inside it. Fill CATCH to 100% = landed! If LINE fills, it escapes.
Fish (🐟 GOBLIN · 🐠 SOLANA · 🐡 PUMPFUN) and pets (🦞 CLAW · 🦀 CLAWD · 🐎 HERMES) give points on the spot. 👢 boot / 🥫 can = ZONK (no points).
Open 🎣 TACKLE BOX to see everything you've caught (fish, pets, even zonk) and toggle a pet to FOLLOW you or send it home. Change gear with 🎣 GEAR.

🏹 COMBAT — THE OUTER WORLDS

GOBLIN VILLAGE & 🎣 ANGLER COVE are safe (no monsters) — 🦅 SKY HAWKS ferry build materials. 👾 Monsters only appear in the OTHER worlds.
Grab a bow at the 🏹 ARMORY first — no weapon = you can't kill monsters! Shops in every world stock stronger bows the deeper you go.
You auto-loose arrows at the nearest 👾. Watch your ❤ HP — hit to 0 = die & respawn. Walk / flee to heal. Big brute monsters fire back!
Kill monsters = +XP / points (big ones give a lot!). Your 🐾 pet & SHAMAN (talk to recruit ⚔) fight with you.
👑 Every world has a MONSTER KING — a huge boss that's tougher each level. Slay it for massive XP + ⌬; it returns after a while.
